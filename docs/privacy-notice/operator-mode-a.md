# Operator Telemetry Notice (Mode A)

**English**

GreenBro Heat Pump Operations - Mode A Telemetry Notice  
We collect technical telemetry from your heat pump equipment to support maintenance and performance optimization. All personal identifiers are pseudonymized within mainland China using HMAC-SHA256 and remain under the control of the China entity. The overseas operations team receives only aggregated technical metrics (temperatures, flows, pressures, energy usage, alerts). No names, contact details, precise locations, or other personal data leave China. For access, correction, or deletion requests, please contact the China operations team via the published hotline.

**中文（审核：李芳 / 法务，2025-11-11）**

GreenBro 热泵运营 — Mode A 遥测通知  
我们仅为维护与性能优化目的收集设备运行遥测数据。所有个人标识信息都会在中国境内通过 HMAC-SHA256 方式进行假名化处理，并由中国实体独立保管。境外运营团队仅会接触汇总后的技术指标（如温度、流量、压力、能耗、告警等），不会接触姓名、联系方式、精确位置或其他个人数据。如需访问、更正或删除相关数据，请通过公示的热线联系中国区运营团队。
